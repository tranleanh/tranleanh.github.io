About Me


I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. Degree, guided by Prof. Dong-Chul Park, a pioneer of Artificial Neural Networks. Also, I am working as a Research Intern at MindinTech (Telechips).

In 2014, I entered Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology and Education (HCMUTE).

In 2017, I became a Teaching Assistant and joined Intelligent Systems Lab at HCMUTE.

In 2018, I graduated, published my first research paper, and worked as an AI Engineer at FPT Software.

In 2019, I moved to South Korea for a Master's degree and got a summer internship at OCST.

Since 2020, I have been working as a Research Intern at MindinTech.

In 2021, I started my Ph.D. journey right after receiving the M.Sc. degree.

I love beauty, sports, singing and drawing.
