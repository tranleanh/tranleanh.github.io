About Me

Hi there! I'm Le-Anh Tran, a Computer Vision Scientist and a Writer for Towards Data Science.

Back in 2014, I enrolled at Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology and Education (HCMUTE). Three years later, I joined Intelligent Systems Lab (ISLab) and became a Teaching Assistant at HCMUTE. In 2018, I obtained my BEng Degree and published my first research paper. During that time, my team (ISLab) participated in the Digital Race Contest organized by FPT Corporation, where afterward I worked as an AI Engineer for one year before moving to South Korea in early 2019 to pursue postgraduate education. In 2019, I had the opportunity to be an intern at OCST Co., Ltd. in Seoul. Since 2020, I've been working as a Research Staff Member at MindinTech, Inc. in Seoul. I started my PhD journey in Spring 2021 and completed it in early 2024. My research interests include Neural Networks, Image Enhancement, Object Detection, and Cluster Analysis.

Apart from my passion for research, I also have a love for sports, singing, and drawing. Taking care of my research is always a top priority for me.
