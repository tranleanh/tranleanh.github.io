About Me


I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. Degree in Computer Vision & Machine Learning at Myongji University, besides, I am also working as an AI Researcher at MindinTech, Inc. (Telechips), South Korea.

I received the B.Eng. Degree in Automation and Control Engineering with Distinction Degree Classification from Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology and Education (HCMUTE), Vietnam, in 2018, and the M.S. Degree in Electronics Engineering from Myongji University, South Korea, in 2021. Earlier in 2017, I was a Teaching Assistant and joined Intelligent Systems Lab, HCMUTE, where I began my research journey and published my first research paper at the end of 2018. From 2018 to 2019, I had been an AI Engineer at the Department of AI Transformation, FPT Software Ltd., Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. So far, I have been working with Prof. Dong-Chul Park, who has over 30-year experience in Machine Learning and had been a member of the Editorial Board for IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks.

I love beauty, melody, sports; and can not fail to mention drawing which is always along with me as a great passion.

Last but not least, I always take care of my research due to my massive love for this work.
